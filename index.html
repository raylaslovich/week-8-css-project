Tips for Astrophotography

The following are just a few tips to make Astro-shots that much easier!

  1. Access to a tripod.
  2. A remote shutter.
  3. A wide angle lens.
  4. Take multipe shots!
Tip Price Reason
Tripod $11.99 Having a stable base is crucial for capturing the night sky.
Remote Shutter $39.99 A remote shutter removes any unwanted "shakiness" from pressing the shutter on the camera body.
Wide angel lens. $400.00 (budget friendly :) ) Having a wide frame is a personal preference, but it allows for capturing more of the night sky.
Multiple shots! Priceless! Practice makes perfect, and one shot just may be the winner out!
